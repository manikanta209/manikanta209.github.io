About

I'm an innovative BIM Engineer recognised for high productivity and efficient delivery. My work sits at the intersection of design and construction — building accurate 3D models, running clash detection, and coordinating between architectural, structural and MEP disciplines so projects move forward without surprises.

With a Civil Engineering degree and hands-on site experience before moving into BIM, I understand what a model needs to mean on the ground. I specialise in Scan-to-BIM — converting LIDAR mesh data into clean, as-built Revit models — and I'm comfortable on cloud collaboration platforms like Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC).
